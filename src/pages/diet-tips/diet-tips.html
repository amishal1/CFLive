Achieving and maintaining a good weight is essential for maintaining good lung f unction and helping withstand infection, and is associated with improved outcomes for people with cystic fibrosis (CF).

Food

Calories are a measure of how much energy a food contains. Dietary fat provides the most energy (calories) in the smallest volume and is the reason that we encourage you to use high fat foods and snacks whenever possible, together with the appropriate number of enzymes.
